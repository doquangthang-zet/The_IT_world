III.         Project Idea

Overview: Now we are citizens of the 21st century-the era of great technological development. Every day, millions of technology projects are implemented to solve common problems and raise living standards. In this section, I will introduce my little idea related to music file processing. Simply put, I am intending to build a computer application whose purpose is to convert the songs of singers to the sounds of different musical instruments such as piano, guitar, trumpet, flute, etc. Not only verbal sounds, but all different types of sounds can be converted to other sounds with the same melody and tempo.

Motivation: This idea came to my mind a few days ago, when I was scouring the internet looking for a new bamboo flute soundtrack for my favorite song. But I did not find any trace and I was thinking: "all sounds and tones are represented by musical notes (basic 7 notes) and computers can digitize their sounds and represent them by another sound's form". In my opinion, this is a very interesting project because I will not need to practice playing the flute anymore, but I can enjoy them easily with just my smartphone. In fact, there are more than 30 million people who love piano pieces, more than 1 billion people who love guitars, and many other musical instruments. Therefore, this can be a revolutionary project for music enthusiasts like me.

Description: This project sounds simple and trivial, but to create a product that satisfies the user, takes a whole process of editing and finishing. This is a new project and probably requires some knowledge of music theory so I cannot say exactly about its specifics, but it is a web app, which means users can use either web or app to take action. There are two main uses of the app: converting audio file formats and converting audio to other audio. Converting file formats is more convenient for users because the application may only accept a specific type of audio file (wav, mp3,...). Specifically, this function allows users to convert between different audio file formats such as from mp3 to wav, even separating the audio from the video or separating the main sound from the background music. There will be an area for users to insert their original audio files and a list of file formats so they can consider choosing the most suitable format. After that, it will take a while to complete the conversion and users can download their files to their computer. The second function is the most interesting part - converting sounds to musical instruments. There will also be an area for inserting original audio files and a diverse list of instruments, in general, the process will be the same as the previous function. Natural language processing and chatbots

Natural language processing is a part of linguistics and artificial intelligence, mainly focusing on analyzing and processing natural language and audio data (natural language processing).. Its goal is to make it possible for humans to naturally communicate with computers without understanding machine language. Currently, this technology is applied in many different fields such as machine translation, spam detection, information extraction, summarization, question answering, handwriting recognition, speech recognition, information mining, etc. (Diksha Khurana ). Machine translation is a technology that converts between different languages ​​like Google Translate to make information accessible and available to everyone. Another technology based on NLP is text categorization which allows users to classify information in a huge collection of information, a common example of this technology is spam detection in email. In addition, information extraction is also an extremely important application. We all know that the world today possesses an almost endless amount of information, information retrieval helps users to extract the necessary information for many different purposes in a short time. Summarization is also a technology to solve information overload problem based on Natural language process. As knowledge acquisition becomes difficult in the age of technology, summarizing data becomes more important than ever. A project in the medical field that is based on Summarization and Information Extract technology is the Linguistic String Project-Medical Language Processor that detects drug side effects (Diksha Khurana ). Parallel to the development of natural language processing is the birth of state-of-art techniques such as Recurrent Neural Networks(RNN's), Long Short-Term Memory networks(LSTM), Attention Mechanism, the BERT model , Transformer-XL, Compressive Transformers (The Current State of the Art in Natural Language Processing (NLP)). These techniques have taken natural language processing to the next level, making for more optimal data storage and management. In addition, many large projects related to natural language processing have been conducted in recent years, marking major changes in this technology. In addition, many large projects related to natural language processing have been conducted in recent years, marking major changes in this technology. Some of them include ACE Powered GDPR Robot Launched by RAVN Systems, Eno A Natural Language Chatbot Launched by Capital One, developing a Conceptual Framework for Medication Therapy Management Research etc. (Diksha Khurana ). The GDPR("General Data Protection Regulation") Robot uses AI techniques to automatically analyze documents and other types of data in any business system which is subject to GDPR rules. It allows users to quickly and easily search, retrieve, flag, classify and report on data mediated to be supersensitive under GDPR (Diksha Khurana ). Eno chatbot is an invention using natural language operating in the banking sector. It allows users to communicate and interact about banking basics in natural language. Medication Therapy Management is also a project that creates many advances in the field of medicine that is powered by natural language. This technology shows us a future where there is no longer a boundary between robots and humans. However, this is the most complex area of artificial intelligence after all, so breakthrough changes will require a lot of research time. Here is where we are at: Capable of imitating consciousness through machines but not capable of understanding what consciousness is (Marche). In the coming years, chatbots will probably be widely applied in many different fields, but making computers fully understand human language could take a century.

Automation

Automation is a technology that uses machines or a series of different machines to operate a certain job that reduces human labor. Automation is said to be able to replace humans in doing heavy jobs with high precision and lower cost than human labor. In addition, the use of machines contributes to improving efficiency in mass production, increasing the speed and quantity of products to serve human needs. Nowadays, automation is applied in almost every aspect of life such as the introduction of dishwashers, auto retail, auto mining, auto surveillance camera, auto highway system, self-driving cars, auto support services and management, especially in the field of goods manufacturing. In the architecture of automated production systems, individual tasks are divided among different operational management areas such as production management, maintenance management, inventory management, quality management, etc. Each part is created using different technologies to form an automatic production line (Thomas Bangemann, 2014). There are a few popular automation technologies today, such as assembly automation that allows automatic assembly of components on a pre-programmed line, which is mainly applied in the automotive, electrical - electronic industries, food production, consumer goods, etc. Another application of automation technology is pick and place robots. These robots mainly perform heavy, repetitive manual tasks in place of humans. There are also many other technologies such as machine tending, material addition, etc. One of the great developments in automation technology in recent years is autonomous vehicles(AV) - vehicles that can move on their own without human assistance. This trend is the result of different research methods such as Keywords Extraction using Bicomb2 software, research hotpots, development trends by CiteSpace V(YOU Dingyi, 2018). This technology has made remarkable progress in three aspects: Environmental Perception, Decision Making, and Motion Control and proves its feasibility in the future (YOU Dingyi, 2018). Environmental Perception aims to sense and detect current traffic information and surrounding obstacles and transmit them to the Decision Making department as input. Currently, this technology is implemented through hardware such as: LIDAR, stereo camera, millimeter-wave radar and V2X communication platform. The Decision Making system will analyze the input information combined with the direction plan, map data, and rules to make decisions for the vehicle to ensure safety, speed, and comfort, and save fuel. This system works on methods like: state machine, decision tree and deep learning. Motion Control system includes two forms: human interaction and self-controlled vehicles. In the near future, perhaps robots will gradually replace most of human jobs from hard labor to small jobs, automated vehicles, automated infrastructure, will gradually appear. Related technologies are artificial intelligence which is a contributing factor to the growth of automation. Although automated machines can do most of the work of humans, it still needs human supervision and products of artificial intelligence industry do not even need the support of humans. People. If the two fields come together, it is possible to create products that change the lives of people around the world.

Automation technologies are believed to bring benefits to the economic sector because they provide a tireless workforce. However, it is the fear of today's human resources, especially those without expertise and technology knowledge. The jobs of these unskilled workers are just basic tasks so they can be completely replaced by machines. Frey and Osborne predict that tellers are among the workers in transportation, logistics, service, and office and administrative occupations - many of the occupations that constitute middle class jobs - that are at the highest risk of being replaced by technology. Overall, they conclude that 47% of total employment in the United States is at high risk of automation (i.e. in an occupation with at least a 70% chance of automation) in the next decade or two (Nzau, 2019). Not only that, automation technology is placing a heavy burden on the environment. This technology uses mainly machines, with minimal human intervention. This led to the creation of more and more factories and the exploitation of fossil fuels. As a result, nature will be more devastated.

This technology plays a core role in modern life, compared to 20 years ago, people no longer have to work hard to find food, thanks to the participation of automation. In daily life, we also use products of this technology without noticing such as dishwashers, cleaning robots, automatic water dispensers, automatic air conditioners, etc. In addition, the outstanding development of this technology also contributes to changing the direction of people's future.
